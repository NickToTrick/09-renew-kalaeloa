Hunt is revitalizing nearly 540 acres of land in Kalaeloa (formerly Naval Air Station Barbers Point) into a mixed-use, master-planned community. These commercial, light industrial and residential projects will create more than 7,000 direct and indirect jobs, more than 1,000 construction jobs, and ultimately 4,000 homes.

Hunt’s Kalaeloa Strategic Implementation Plan is an important public-private partnership between Hunt, the City & County of Honolulu, and the State of Hawai’i which will deliver a vibrant and revitalized Kalaeloa as envisioned in the State’s Kalaeloa Master Plan.

Hunt is committed to being responsible stewards of the land, creating value for the entire community.
